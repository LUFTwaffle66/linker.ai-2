# Notification Types for AI-Focused Freelancer Marketplace

This document outlines the different types of notifications that will be sent to freelancers and clients on the platform.

---

### For Freelancers

**1. Project & Opportunity Notifications**
- **New Project Matching Your Skills:** Alerts when a new project is posted that matches the freelancer's profile and skill set.
- **Invitation to a Project:** When a client directly invites them to apply for a project.

**2. Proposal & Application Notifications**
- **Proposal Submitted:** Confirmation that their proposal has been successfully sent.
- **Client Viewed Your Proposal:** An alert to let them know a client has seen their application.
- **New Message Regarding Proposal:** When a client sends a message to discuss their proposal.
- **Proposal Accepted:** The exciting news that their proposal has been chosen.
- **Proposal Declined:** To provide closure and allow them to move on to other opportunities.
- **Offer Received:** When a client sends a formal offer to start a contract.

**3. Project & Contract Notifications**
- **Contract Started:** Confirmation that the offer has been accepted and the project is officially underway.
- **New Message from Client:** For ongoing communication during a project.
- **Project Requirements Updated:** If the client makes changes to the project scope.
- **Contract Ended:** When the project is completed and the contract is closed.
- **Review Received:** An alert that the client has left them feedback.

**4. Payment & Financial Notifications**
- **Upfront Payment Secured:** Confirmation that the client has paid the initial 50% to start the project.
- **Final Payment Released:** Notification that the remaining 50% has been paid upon project completion.
- **Withdrawal Successful:** When a withdrawal to their bank account has been processed.

---

### For Clients

**1. Project Posting & Freelancer Discovery Notifications**
- **Project Posted Successfully:** Confirmation that their project is live on the marketplace.
- **New Proposal Received:** An alert for each new proposal on their project.
- **AI-Suggested Freelancers:** Proactive suggestions of top AI experts who would be a great fit for their project.
- **Invited Freelancer Responded:** When a freelancer they invited has either applied or declined.

**2. Proposal & Hiring Notifications**
- **New Message from a Freelancer:** For any questions or discussions about a project.
- **Freelancer Accepted/Declined Offer:** Notification of the freelancer's decision on a contract offer.
- **Reminder to Review Proposals:** If a project has several proposals waiting for review.

**3. Project & Contract Notifications**
- **Contract Started:** Confirmation that the freelancer has accepted the offer.
- **New Message from Freelancer:** For ongoing project communication.
- **Reminder to Pay Upfront:** A prompt to pay the initial 50% to get the project started.
- **Reminder to Pay Final Amount:** A notification to pay the remaining 50% once the project is complete.
- **Contract Ended:** When the project is marked as complete.
- **Reminder to Leave a Review:** A prompt to provide feedback on the freelancer's work.

**4. Payment & Financial Notifications**
- **Upfront Payment Successful:** Confirmation that the initial 50% has been paid.
- **Final Payment Successful:** Confirmation that the remaining 50% has been paid.
- **Invoice Received:** For any direct payments or platform fees.
